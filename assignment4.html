04｜Exquisite Corpse

Reflection


I found that the most challenging part of learning p5 is figuring out how to translate the imagined compositions, colors, and aesthetics in my mind into actual code. It often feels like my ideas outpace my technical skills. This was especially true in the Exquisite Corpse project, where blending three distinct aesthetics on a single canvas posed a significant challenge.

Since I wasn’t completely satisfied with the initial result of this project, I decided to revisit it using the bouncing ball effect we learned in class. This is my final outcome!
